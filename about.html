About Display Community

Celebrating creativity in libraries everywhere.

What is Display Community?

Display Community is a place for library workers to share, discover, and celebrate creative library displays from around the world. Our goal is to create a searchable source of inspiration where staff can find ideas, learn from other libraries, and showcase the creativity happening in their own communities.

Who is this for?

Display Community is designed for librarians, library staff, library volunteers, and anyone interested in creating engaging library displays.

Submission Guidelines

To maintain a high-quality community resource, all submissions are reviewed before being published.

  • Displays should be created for a library or library-related space.
  • Photos should show the completed display clearly.
  • Descriptions should include useful information such as materials, process, and inspiration.
  • Content should be appropriate for a professional library setting.

What Makes a Great Submission?

Great displays often include:

  • Creative use of space
  • Clear connection between design and collection
  • Helpful information for other library workers
  • Unique ideas that inspire others
